About URCW

Established in 2006, Dr. Umayal Ramanathan College for Women, affiliated with Alagappa University, holds a A grade accreditation from NAAC, with 3.22 points out of 4 in its second cycle. In March 2025, it gained recognition under sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act 1956. Situated in a tranquil 15-acre campus at the heart of Karaikudi, the college is easily accessible.
